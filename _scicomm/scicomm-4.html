--- title: "Podcast: Genetics in Your World" excerpt: "I conduct research and interviews for the GSA's Genetics in Your World podcast." collection: scicomm --- As part of the Genetic Society of America's Early Career Leadership Program (Multimedia Subcommittee), I help research for, interview for, and produce episodes for the podcast "Genetics in Your World". We aim to communicate current genetics research to a broad scientific audience by interviewing the authors of exciting new papers in G3 and Genetics.
